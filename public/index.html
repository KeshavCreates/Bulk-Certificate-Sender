📧 Setup Your Email for Sending

Before sending certificates, you need to generate an App Password from your email provider. Follow the steps below.

1

Open App Passwords Page

Click the button below to open Google's App Passwords page. You must be signed in to your Google account.

🔗 Open Google App Passwords

⚠️ You need 2-Step Verification enabled on your Google account to see this page.

2

Create a New App Password

On the App Passwords page:

  1. Type a name like "CertifyPro" in the app name field
  2. Click "Create"
  3. Google will generate a 16-character password (e.g., abcd efgh ijkl mnop)
3

Copy & Save the Password

Important: Copy the generated password immediately and save it somewhere safe (e.g., a Notepad file). Google will not show it again after you close the dialog.

⚡ Tip: You'll paste this password in the next step as your "App Password". Do NOT use your regular Gmail password.
4

Use It in the Next Step

In the next step, enter:

  • Email Address: Your full Gmail address (e.g., you@gmail.com)
  • App Password: The 16-character password you just generated

That's it! You're ready to send certificates.

📌 Using Outlook or another provider?

For Outlook, use your regular email and password with SMTP host smtp-mail.outlook.com (port 587). For other providers, check their SMTP documentation.
